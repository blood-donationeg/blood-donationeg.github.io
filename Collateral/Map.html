Where can you donate?

it is immensely crucial to place your donation in a verified and trusted health institute that is under the jurisdiction of the Egyptian Ministry Of Health so that you are sure that you will be dealing with a well-trained doctor that is qualified to handle your blood donation process, and so that you can be completely certain that your blood will actually be used to help people who actually need it.

We value your time and effort, so we made it super easy for you to find out the nearest verified blood donation institute. All you have to do is to go to the map below, and click on the nearest place to your residence and you will find it's exact location.
